## PokéRestore

PokéRestore is a Pokémon card cleaning and restoration service for collectors in France and across Europe. I created the complete product experience—from the brand and interface to the commerce flow—and I personally carry out every restoration.

The site makes a specialist service feel clear and trustworthy: collectors can understand the process, choose a service, submit the details of their cards, pay securely, and follow their order from shipment through return delivery.

## What I Did

- Product strategy and service design
- Brand identity and visual direction
- UX and UI design
- Next.js development
- Bilingual French and English experience
- Order flow and customer tracking
- The card cleaning and restoration work itself

## Customer Experience

The marketing site introduces the restoration process, builds confidence with clear service information, and guides collectors toward an order without overwhelming them.

![PokéRestore homepage](/images/pokerestore/home.webp)

The order flow captures the information needed to assess and process each card, then moves customers into a secure Stripe Checkout flow. After payment, they can track their restoration through each stage—from shipment and reception to completion and return delivery.

![PokéRestore order flow](/images/pokerestore/order-flow.webp)

## Built for France and Europe

PokéRestore is fully localized for its audiences, with French for customers in France and English for the rest of Europe. The project includes locale-aware SEO, hreflang metadata, a sitemap, Open Graph images, and Markdown content for the blog and legal pages.

![PokéRestore blog](/images/pokerestore/blog.webp)

## Technical Stack

- Next.js 16, React 19, TypeScript, and Tailwind CSS
- next-intl for bilingual routing and SEO
- better-auth with passwordless magic-link sign-in
- Drizzle ORM with libSQL / Turso-compatible SQLite
- Stripe Checkout and webhooks for EUR payments
- Resend for transactional email
- Markdown-powered blog and legal content

## Operations Dashboard

Alongside the customer experience, I built an owner-only dashboard for managing the service: updating order statuses, uploading reception and finished-card photos, adding tracking information, and issuing additional charges when needed.
