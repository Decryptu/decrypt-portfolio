CapybarIA is a data visualization platform aimed at analyzing the artificial intelligence ecosystem. It features information such as the aggregated market cap of AI companies, rankings of open-source natural language processing engines, trends in developer activity on Github, and much more. The platform is developed in React and uses ECharts.
